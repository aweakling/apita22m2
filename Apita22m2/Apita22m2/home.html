Elden Ring Synopsis

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is known for its challenging gameplay, vast open world, and intricate lore crafted by Hidetaka Miyazaki in collaboration with George R. R. Martin.
