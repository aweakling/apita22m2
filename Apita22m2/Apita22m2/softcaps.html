Main Stats in Elden Ring:

  • Vigor: Determines maximum HP.
  • Mind: Governs FP and magic abilities.
  • Endurance: Controls Stamina.
  • Strength: Influences physical attack power.
  • Dexterity: Affects agility and precision.
  • Intelligence: Enhances spellcasting.
  • Faith: Proficiency in miracles.
  • Arcane: Affinity for eldritch powers.
